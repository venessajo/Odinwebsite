Tips to keep in mind.
This image displays the number one rule when working on your computer. Keep your area clean in order to avoid accidents.
The next thing to keep in mind is caffeine. Give yourself a treat every once in a while.
It's important to take a break every 15 minutes. This can help to clear your mind and keep you on task.
Lastly, don't be hard on yourself. Everyone gets stuck and it's important to stay positive and remember why you started.
